Orange Gelatin Salad Recipe

Description

A super easy orange Jell-O recipe that takes a new spin on fruit salad!

Ingredients

  • 1, 11 ounce can mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1, 8 ounce can crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1, 6 ounce package orange flavored Jell-O mix
  • 16 ounces cottage cheese
  • 8 ounces frozen whipped topping, thawed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine oranges, pineapple, and gelatin in mixing bowl. Mix well and let chill for 30 minutes.
  3. Add cottage cheese to mixture. Stir to combine.
  4. Fold in whipped topping. Chill and serve.
