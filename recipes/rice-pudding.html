Rice Pudding

Description

Rice pudding is the beautiful combination of creamy rice, milk, and egg cooked into a thick, soft, and delicious dessert. Further enhanced by the addition raisins, sugar, and vanilla, this is a dessert that you must try.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of cold water
  • 3/4 cup of uncooked rice
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/3 cup of white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 2/3 cup of raisins
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook rice for 20 minutes or until tender
  3. Transfer cooked rice to clean saucepan. Mix in milk, sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until thick and creamy
  4. Mix in beaten egg and raisins. Continue to cook for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla until combined
  5. Serve warm
